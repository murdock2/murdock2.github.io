Austin Murdock, PhD

Hello! I am a researcher specializing in Internet measurement and cybersecurity.

My PhD dissertation topic was Internet-wide IPv6 scanning. I was a student in UC Berkeley's Computer Security research group, advised by Vern Paxson. I was also a member of the Center for Evidence-based Security Research (CESR) and ICSI's Networking and Security group. Prior to Berkeley, my research focused on Network Intrusion Detection and Prevention at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology Lincoln Laboratory and platform security at the Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory.

After completing my PhD, I have served as the principal investigator on several DOD grants:

  • U.S. Army SBIR Phase II (2023 - 2025)
  • U.S. Air Force SBIR Phase I (2024)
  • U.S. Army SBIR Phase I (2022 - 2023)

I have won awards based on my work, including:

  • University of Maryland Baltimore County Rising Star Award (2024)
  • U.S. Army xTechSearch 6 Award (2022)
  • Global Cyber Innovation Summit Disrupt 8 Award (2022)

I am the founder of the cybersecurity company SixMap.
